You have just scanned the QR code of a Twomes p1-reader. Above, you can see a photo of the p1-reader. You may have also received a splitter (bottom left) and a USB power adapter (bottom right).

We will provide you with instructions on how to install the device with your smart meter. If you have any questions after reading these instructions, please first check the FAQ section. There you will also find information on how to get assistance if you are unable to proceed.

1. Connect the USB power adapter if necessary

For the most modern smart meters, the p1-reader is powered by the smart meter itself. Older models require an external power adapter.

  • If you have received 2 USB power adapters from us, use one of them for the p1-reader.
  • If you have received 1 USB power adapter from us, it depends on whether you have also received an OpenTherm Monitor from us (a small white box with 2 green screw terminal connectors).
    • If you have received an OpenTherm Monitor, use the USB power adapter for that.
    • If you have not received an OpenTherm Monitor, then you should connect the USB power adapter to the p1-reader.
  • If you have not received a USB power adapter from us, it means that we have detected that your smart meter is of the modern type that can provide sufficient power for the p1-reader.

If the USB power adapter is required for the smart meter, insert the micro-USB plug into the side of the p1-reader (it only fits one way), connect the USB plug to the power adapter, and finally plug the power adapter into an available electrical outlet near your smart meter.

2. Connect the P1 cable

Insert the detachable plug of the p1-reader into the P1 port of your smart meter. You should feel a distinct click, indicating that the plug is properly connected.
The P1 port is located on the front or bottom of many smart meters. Sometimes there is an easily removable cover in front of it.


If your P1 port is already occupied, use the supplied splitter: first remove the plug that occupies the P1 port, then insert the detachable plug of the splitter into the P1 port, and finally insert the two detachable plugs into the 2 available ports of the splitter.


Do not place the p1-reader right next to your Wi-Fi router, as it may interfere with communication with the other modules later on.


You have connected the p1-reader.

3. Enable Bluetooth

Make sure Bluetooth is enabled on your smartphone. This is required for the next step.


Proceed with the "Ok, I've done it" button below in the app to complete the installation of the p1-reader.
